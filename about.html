#About US

About Our Farmers

We are committed to working with local farmers to bring you the highest quality tubo rice, mangoes, and corn. Our farmers are experienced and passionate about their craft, and they use sustainable farming practices to produce the best possible crops.

Rice

Iloilo, a province in the Western Visayas region of the Philippines, is renowned for its picturesque landscapes, rich cultural heritage, and, of course, its exquisite rice. Rice cultivation has been a mainstay of Iloilo's economy for centuries, and the province is now recognized as a major rice producer in the country.

Tubo

Our tubo rice is a premium variety of rice that is known for its long, slender grains and delicate flavor. It is grown in the fertile Iloilo Valley, and it is harvested by hand to ensure its quality.

Mangoes

Our mangoes are grown in the tropical climate of Iloilo City, and they are known for their sweetness and juiciness. We pick our mangoes at the peak of ripeness to ensure that they are the best possible flavor.

Corn

Our corn is grown in the same fields as our tubo rice, and it is harvested by hand to ensure its quality. We use our corn to make a variety of products, including cornmeal, tortillas, and chips.

Thank you for choosing our farmers!

We are proud to partner with our farmers to bring you the best possible tubo rice, mangoes, and corn. We are committed to sustainable farming practices, and we are dedicated to providing our customers with the highest quality products.
